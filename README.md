# 🚀 StriveX Sales Performance Intelligence Dashboard  

🔍 Turning 9,500+ rows of raw sales data into actionable business insights through Power BI

---

## 📌 Business Problem  
In a competitive retail environment, businesses often struggle to answer critical questions:  
- Which regions are driving the most revenue?  
- Which products are underperforming?  
- How does profit vary across different sales channels?  
- Are there seasonal trends affecting sales performance?  

Despite having data, the lack of centralized and interactive analytics makes decision-making slow and inefficient.  

👉 This project solves this by building a data-driven Sales Intelligence Dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Objective  
- Analyze 9,500+ sales records to uncover trends and patterns  
- Track key KPIs like Total Sales, Operating Profit, Units Sold, and Profit Margin  
- Enable stakeholders to make fast, data-driven decisions  
- Provide an interactive and visually intuitive dashboard  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies  
- Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)  
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)  
- DAX (Data Modeling & Calculations)  

---

## 📊 Key KPIs  
- 💰 Total Sales: 700M  
- 📈 Operating Profit: 332.13M  
- 📦 Units Sold: 2M  
- 💲 Price per Unit: 45.22  
- 📊 Operating Margin: 42%  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights  

### 🔹 Sales Trend Analysis  
- Peak sales observed in July–August (~95M) indicating strong seasonal demand  
- Noticeable dip in October (~64M) suggesting potential off-season trend  

👉 Insight: Optimize inventory and marketing campaigns around peak months  

---

### 🔹 Regional Performance  
- West region contributes ~30% of total sales (highest)  
- Other regions contribute less comparatively  

👉 Insight: Expand in high-performing regions and improve weaker markets  

---

### 🔹 Product Performance  
- Men’s Street Footwear leads (~0.59M units sold)  
- Some categories underperform significantly  

👉 Insight: Focus on high-demand products and optimize low performers  

---

### 🔹 Retailer Contribution  
- Top retailers contribute 0.6M+ units while others lag  

👉 Insight: Strengthen top retailer partnerships and optimize weaker channels  

---

### 🔹 Profitability Analysis  
- Strong Operating Margin of 42%  
- Profit varies across categories and regions  

👉 Insight: Scale high-margin products to maximize profitability
